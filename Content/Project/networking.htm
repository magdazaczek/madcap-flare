Networking

Business networking is the act of keeping positive relationships with clients and other professionals in your industry or work area. You never know when your contact list will prove useful.

You may ask Why do I need it?The answer is simple. It's essential to share important insights, stay updated on industry developments and achieve business goals. For many people, networking stands for an excellence opportunity to receive valuable career guidance or learn about job opportunities.

How to start

If you have no experience networking and want to start building your reputation and share information with others, the following steps are for you.

  1. Define your work area.

  2. Look for some events around, such as conferences, workshops, campaigns, etc.

  3. Do some research on available opportunities online.

  4. Join Facebook and LinkedIn groups for professionals from your industry.

  5. Start networking! Take advantage of every opportunity and don't be afraid. If you're looking for some ice breaker questions, check the Speed Networking Questions: 100 (Actually Interesting) Things to Ask article.
