Remote working model

For many people, working remotely is a dream come true. The popularity of 100% remote positions appeared, of course, during the pandemic time. Many workplaces weren't ready for such a working model, but there was no choice.

After the pandemic time, some companies decided to close their offices and switch to 100% remote work.

People are interested in remote positions for various reasons:

  • They don't want to commute to work and waste time.

  • They can save more money because they don't have to buy bus tickets or fuel their cars so often.

  • They feel more comfortable working from their home.

  • They often are flexible to set their own working hours, which is a big benefit for people who have children.

What is interesting, some employees would give up pay for a possibility to work remotely. For more information, see the Many Workers Willing to Give Up Pay for Remote Work article.
