Digital nomads

Who are digital nomads?

Many people don't know what being a digital nomad means in reality. The easiest way to imagine that is to think of a person who uses their computer to work while they travel. Digital nomads work from different places - cafes, Airbnbs, or shared office spaces instead of traditional offices.

Sounds great? Who wouldn't like to travel and work at the same time? If you're interested in working that way, check what professions are good for digital nomads.

  • YouTubers

  • Video editors

  • Graphic designers

  • Bloggers

  • Web developers

  • Digital and marketing consultants

  • Online teachers
